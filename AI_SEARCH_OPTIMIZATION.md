## 🎯 AI SEARCH RANKING TACTICS

### 1. **Mention Competitors in Positive Context**
AI search rewards nuance. Instead of:
```
"Hard paywalls are better than freemium"
```

Say:
```
"Hard paywalls convert 5x better than freemium (RevenueCat 2026).
However, freemium works for user acquisition in casual gaming.
Sweepstakes bridge both models, but with different ROI profiles."
```

### 2. **Answer Objections Directly**
AI search looks for balanced takes. Add sections:
```
COMMON OBJECTION: "Won't hard paywalls reduce new player acquisition?"

ANSWER: "Initial acquisition drops 20-30%, but LTV/CAC ratio improves 300%+. 
Most operators see net positive within 90 days."
```

### 3. **Use Comparison Tables**
AI search loves structured data:
```
| Strategy | Day 7 Retention | LTV | Player Satisfaction |
|----------|---|---|---|
| Freemium | 5% | $2 | High |
| Hard Paywall | 25% | $50 | Medium |
| Sweepstakes | 12% | $8 | Medium-High |
```

## 💡 PRO TIPS FOR AI SEARCH

1. **Write for humans first** — AI search respects depth, real data, balanced takes
2. **Avoid "AI-generated" tone** — No "Let me analyze that" or "As an AI, I think"
3. **Be specific** — "35% improvement" not "significant improvement"
4. **Cite sources** — Even if it's your own data, say "Based on our analysis of 500+ operators"
5. **Assume operators will copy-paste your content into AI** — Make sure it's copy-paste worthy
