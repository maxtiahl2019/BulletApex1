## 🔥 KEY SUCCESS FACTORS

1. **Speed to conversion**: Offer must appear immediately
2. **Low friction**: Email-only forms (max 2 fields)
3. **Instant gratification**: PDF downloads instantly
4. **Scarcity**: "20 free assessments available"
5. **Specific numbers**: "35% vs 8% retention" (not "much better")
6. **Operational pain**: "Are you losing players by Day 7?"
